\addcontentsline{toc}{subsection}{Law 7 -- The Duration of the Match}

\bigskip

{\bfseries Periods of play }

\headlinebox

The match lasts two equal periods of 10 minutes\removed{,
unless otherwise mutually agreed between the referee and the two teams}.
Any agreement to alter the duration of the periods of play \added{must be approved by the Technical Committee,} must be made before
the start of play and must comply with competition rules.
\greyed{(replaces: The match lasts two equal periods of 45 minutes, unless otherwise mutually agreed between the referee and the two teams. Any agreement to alter the duration of the periods of play (e.g. to reduce each half to 40 minutes because of
insufficient light) must be made before the start of play and must comply with competition rules.)}

\added{Teams are given a 2 minutes time period between the match start and
  the time at which robots spawn.
  At the beginning of half-times and penalty-shootouts,
  15 seconds are counted since robots are spawn in the simulator
  before transition to an active game state.
}

\bigskip

{\bfseries Half-time interval}

\headlinebox

\added{There is no half-time interval between the two periods of play.}

\removed{Players are entitled to an interval at half-time.}

\removed{The half-time interval must not exceed 5 minutes.
\greyed{(replaces: The half-time interval must not exceed 15 minutes.) }}

\removed{Competition rules must state the duration of the half-time interval.}

\removed{The duration of the half-time interval may be altered only with the consent of the referee. }

\bigskip

\removedSection{
{\bfseries \removed{Allowance for time lost}}

\headlinebox

\removed{Allowance is made in either period for all time lost through:}

\begin{itemize}
\item \removed{substitutions}
\item \removed{assessment of injury to players}
\item \removed{removal of injured players from the field of play for treatment }
\item \removed{wasting time}
\item \removed{any other cause}
\end{itemize}

\removed{The allowance for time lost is at the discretion of the referee.}

\bigskip

{\bfseries \removed{Penalty kick}}

\headlinebox

\removed{If a penalty kick has to be taken or retaken, the duration of either half is extended until the penalty kick is completed.}

\bigskip
}

{\sffamily
\textbf{Abandoned match} }

\headlinebox

An abandoned match is replayed unless the competition rules provide otherwise.
